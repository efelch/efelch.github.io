Experience

I've been a career software engineer since 2000 — working across the full stack in a variety of languages and frameworks, while specializing within the Java/Spring ecosystem. The achievement I am most proud of is developing an onboarding program that enables a new hire to make meaningful contributions to the team within a week, while learning about the company, our standards, and best practices, and having a great time while doing so.

I'm also a maker of things — a woodworker, a sculptor, a Halloween and Christmas prop maker, and a hobbyist game developer. I love to learn how to build new things, and I love sharing what I've learned with others.
